California Assembly

The California Assembly consists of 80 members. Controlling 80 counties. Assembly members can serve 2 years in one term. Assembly members can only serve 12 years in total between the Assembly and Senate. This rule was created with Proposition 28 in 2012 which limits members of the assembly to only serve a total of 12 years between the two chambers or within one of the chambers. If an Assembly member were to only serve in the Assembly they could serve 6 terms.
